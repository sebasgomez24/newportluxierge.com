About Newport Luxierge




Newport Luxierge Electronic Solutions came to be as a collaboration of professionals who have spent decades dealing with electronic systems and watching their evolution. We have worked in a variety of environments and installed a wide selection of electronic systems. Through our extensive involvements, we have learned one very important lesson: An electronics system is only as good as the user experience it provides.


Using this mantra, we take the time to understand the needs and desires of every client we encounter, as well as their level of technical proficiency. NLES is committed to exploring the gamut of solutions and selecting the one that best suits our clientele and their specific needs.


Most companies believe that their job is complete on the day their clients begin to use the system. At NLES, we understand that this is just the beginning. Our obligation to our clients doesn’t end with the receipt of a check, it carries on for as long as our systems are in use and our clients are around to enjoy them. At NLES, we understand that this is just the start of our relationship.
