ACCESS CONTROL
Welcome to the world of intelligent access control. Our systems go far beyond simply unlocking a door when someone enters the appropriate code. When an authorized user attempts access, the doors will be unlocked, lights will turn on, HVAC will adjust, alarm automatically disarms, and a video camera takes a photo. All user activity is logged for review at any time, so you can make sure that people who enter the structure are who they are supposed to be . 
On top of that, we have a more advanced user authentication process that most other solutions. A simple access code won’t necessarily get you in to the structure. Unless you are authorized to be there at that specific date and time with permissions to disarm the security, access is denied.  This means that you can control when people can come and go. This proves especially use when dealing with multiple vendors and service providers. You can even create a temporary access for a person to enter during a specific window, and never gain entry again. 
As if that wasn’t good enough, all of these features can be managed from anywhere in the world, so long as you have a network connection. That means that you can create and modify user permissions from your hotel in Europe or while sitting on the beach in Hawaii.  The ability to easily dynamically control access to your property is invaluable. Not only is this great for safety and security, but convenience and peace of mind.
